App name:
Smokeless - quit smoking

Description:
Smokeless helps you quit smoking, measuring your financial and health improvements since the day you quitted.

See in an effective and attractive way:
1. how long you've not been smoking
2. how much money you've saved since you quitted
3. the benefits of quitting for your health and body.

Get informed by local notifications when your body reaches a new benefit.
